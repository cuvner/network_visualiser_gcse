How to use

  • Add routers, switches, WAPs, servers, computers, printers, and phones.
  • Add an ISP node when you want to show the router linking out to the provider.
  • Drag devices to move them around the LAN canvas.
  • Use connect mode to join devices with UTP, Wi-Fi, or fibre optic links.
  • Build a sensible LAN: for example, desktops usually connect to a switch, and laptops may connect through a WAP.
